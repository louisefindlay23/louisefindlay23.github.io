The Rogue's Odyssey is a fantasy poem that was developed after taking on a challenge to create a short story inspired by Robert Frost's "Stopping by Woods on a Snowy Evening." The story took on a poetic lilt and transformed into a poem.

I discovered that DreamWorlds Publishing had an open call for fantasy works for their upcoming anthology, Dreamless Roads and submitted my poem for consideration. Jan Hawke (CEO of DreamWorlds Publishing) loved it and sent it editor, Sue Bridgwater to be polished.

The Dreamless Roads anthology is available from Amazon in print and eBook form. The Rogue's Odyssey seperately can be purchased from all good eBook retailers.
