In my first year at college, my lecturer announced an opportunity to volunteer for the Aberdeen Cyrenians (a homeless charity in Aberdeen) to take photographs and videos for a few of their events. I saw it as an ideal way to expand my skillset and gain some experience in digital media.

The Baker Hughes 10k run was the event I chose to attend. There were many runners each sponsered by a company, organisation or the public.
