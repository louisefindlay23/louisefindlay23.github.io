I'm a web designer currently studying for my HND at North East Scotland College in Aberdeen. My speciality is getting to the heart of what my clients want and incorprating it into my designs.

I have experience with the framework, Foundation and content management system, WordPress to speed up development and make it easier for clients to make changes themselves if they so desire.
