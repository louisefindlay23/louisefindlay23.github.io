Details of Development

Tools & Technologies Used

Aberdeen Concert Band (a music charity and teaching band) needed to their existing website redesigned to be a hub for their events and to attract new members.

I replaced their outdated blog with an events page to highlight the different types of events they offer: concerts and buskings and added new media (concert videos and audio) to showcase their talents which resulted in a 50% increase in pageviews.

I also transferred the site from WordPress.com to Flywheel to provide the support and security they desired.
