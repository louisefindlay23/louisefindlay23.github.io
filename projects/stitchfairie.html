Jane (The Stitch Fairie) wanted a WordPress website with a booking plugin that had PayPal integration and a dropdown menu for events at her two venues.

Upon speaking with her, I ascertained she needed an events and ticketing plugin rather than a booking plugin which are used for appointment-based events.

Accordingly, I set up Tribe Commerce integration with the Events Calendar Plus and Events Ticket plugins so the dropdown menu for events at both of her venues could be achieved through map views.

Jane wanted ongoing support to manage and maintain her website so she could concentrate on her sewing so I created a bespoke care plan for her.

As a result, I assisted her on creating her Facebook Page.
