Arteriale is a soft furnishings company that was in need of a website to direct newspaper ads to.

The client's requirements were to have a professional email address she could use, a landing page to direct customers to call her for a quote and assistance choosing her domain name.

The solution involved setting up GSuite email and developing a landing page. She requested a drag and drop interface so I developed a landing page using WordPress and the Elementor page builder and hosted it using Flywheel, a managed WordPress host so she wouldn't have to worry about hacking or malware.
