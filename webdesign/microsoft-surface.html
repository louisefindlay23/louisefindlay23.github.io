Languages and Frameworks Used

This website project required a framework and there were several that I was allowed to use. I wanted one that didn't require me to be locked into a template and had simple built-in responsiveness. I chose Foundation for Sites by Zurb. They have great documentation that details all their pre-defined classes.

Using their top-bar, title-area, and top-bar section classes, I could easily create a basic navigation bar while tweaking them to suit the website.

Having testimonials of user experiences in each target market segment was important to achieve the key goal of driving those people to buy Surface products. With Foundation, just the HTML5 blockquote and cite tags created beautiful quote styling.
