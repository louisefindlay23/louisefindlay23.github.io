In this group project, I was in charge of facilitating the communication between our clients, Jonathan Bennett (head of SilverNote) and Tony Young (Curriculum and Quality Manager for Music at North East Scotland College), the artists of SilverNote and the rest of the team.

From speaking with the artists themselves, I learned that they didn't want their music hosted on a website but linked to the streaming services they already used so that they could earn revenue.

I used the window.open JavaScript method to create popup music players (that were embedded iframes of the artists' songs) from various streaming sites such as Soundcloud, Deezer, Spotify and Apple Music. These were contained in the music streaming logos present on each artist's profile.
